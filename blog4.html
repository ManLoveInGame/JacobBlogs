嵐山.京都

Arashiyama.Kyoto

嵐山.京都 Arashiyama.Kyoto

October 15TH 。 Japan 。 Kyoto 。 Arashiyama

Overview

We began our day by taking a train from 嵐山 to 龜岡站, enjoying the scenic ride. Upon arrival, we took a leisurely walk around the area, soaking in the beautiful surroundings. After our stroll, we returned to 嵐山 for a relaxed afternoon. With the end of our trip approaching, we decided not to overexert ourselves and instead enjoyed a chill walk and some delicious local food. This laid-back day allowed us to appreciate the serene beauty of the area without rushing, making it a perfect way to wind down our travels.

Tags

Japan Food See Clear Peace

10:15 AM Breakfast at Japanese KFC

At 10:15 AM, we visited a Japanese KFC, where I was surprised to find that the shop opened later than expected, reminiscent of local customs back in Hong Kong. The KFC experience in Japan is unique, and I was particularly impressed by the taste of the chicken. It was incredibly juicy and fresh, showcasing excellent cooking skills that set it apart from typical fast food. If I were a local, I would happily eat here daily, enjoying the delicious flavors that make KFC a beloved choice in Japan.

11:45 PM Scenic Ride on the 嵯峨野嵐山小火車

At 11:45 AM, we arrived at the 嵯峨野嵐山小火車 (Sagano Scenic Railway) and enjoyed breathtaking views of the lush forest along the route. The train ride, which stretches 7.3 kilometers from 嵯峨 to 龜岡, offers a unique perspective of Japan's natural beauty, winding through the picturesque 保津川 gorge. As we traveled, we appreciated the peaceful moments amidst the stunning scenery, making it a perfect way to unwind and soak in the tranquility of the area before concluding our trip.

1:15 AM Walk from 馬堀 to 龜岡

At 1:15 PM, we began our walk from 馬堀 back to 龜岡, enjoying the scenic views of Japanese farms along the way. The lush fields and rural landscapes brought a refreshing sense of nature, allowing us to appreciate the tranquility of the countryside. This leisurely stroll provided a perfect opportunity to connect with the natural beauty of Japan as we made our way back to the train station, reflecting on the peaceful moments we had experienced during our trip.

2:10 PM Lunch at 嵐山 Main Shopping Area

At 2:10 PM, we arrived at the 嵐山 main shopping area and enjoyed a delightful lunch featuring a fusion of flavors. We tasted 燒賣 (shumai), a type of steamed dumpling, along with refreshing ice cream and a peach drink. Additionally, we savored some delicious Japanese-style fried chicken. The vibrant atmosphere of the shopping district, filled with various food stalls and shops, made our meal even more enjoyable as we soaked in the local culture and culinary delights. This experience was a perfect blend of traditional and modern Japanese cuisine, making it a highlight of our day.

4:15 PM Relaxing Walk Around 嵐山

At 5:15 PM, we arrived at Fushimi Arashiyama Taisha, eager to explore its famous torii gates. As night approached, we realized that the temple lacked sufficient lighting, prompting us to start our descent before it got too dark. Despite the limited time, we found joy in our hike up the mountain, taking in the stunning views and the serene atmosphere. The beauty of the mountain, adorned with thousands of vermilion gates winding through the forest, created a magical experience that highlighted the unique charm of this iconic shrine.
