柴又.東京 Shibamata.Tokyo

October 13TH 。 Japan 。 Tokyo 。 Shibamata

Overview

We began our day in Shibamata, a charming area famous for its historic Taishakuten Temple. The local community has successfully promoted the area, enhancing its appeal with unique marketing efforts. We enjoyed the nostalgic atmosphere, exploring traditional shops and tasting local delicacies that reflect the culture and history of this quaint neighborhood.

Next, we visited TeamLab Planets in Toyosu, where we immersed ourselves in stunning digital art installations. Each exhibit offered a captivating experience, allowing us to relax and engage with the artwork in a unique way. The combination of technology and creativity made for a truly memorable afternoon, showcasing the modern artistic landscape of Japan.

11:00 AM at 柴又

At 11:00 AM, we arrived in Shibamata to explore its historic structures, shops, and statues. The area is renowned for Shibamata Taishakuten, a temple founded in 1629, which showcases traditional Japanese architecture and craftsmanship. As we wandered through the streets, I was impressed by how well the traditional elements have been preserved amidst modern life. The local shops offered unique souvenirs and delicious snacks, reflecting the area's rich cultural heritage. This blend of history and contemporary vibrancy made our visit to Shibamata a truly memorable experience.

12:10 AM At 柴又帝釋天

At 12:10 PM, we arrived at Shibamata Taishakuten, where visitors are encouraged to follow the temple's rules, enhancing the sense of respect and tranquility. The historic temple exuded an authentic atmosphere, showcasing traditional Japanese architecture that left a lasting impression. Inside, we discovered a serene garden, classic Japanese-style houses, and intricate sculptural drawings that invite contemplation. This peaceful environment allowed us to calm our minds and appreciate the beauty of our surroundings, truly embodying the essence of Japan's cultural heritage.

3:15 PM Immersive Experience at TeamLab Planets

At 3:15 PM, we arrived at TeamLab Planets in Toyosu, where we were instantly transported into a mesmerizing world of art and technology. Fortunately, the rainy weather meant we could enter without any waiting time, thanks to our advance booking. The installations offered an impressive sensory experience, allowing us to engage with the artwork in unique ways, such as walking through water and interacting with stunning digital displays. Each exhibit felt like a journey into a different realm, making our visit both relaxing and exhilarating as we immersed ourselves in the creativity of teamLab.

5:30 PM Evening in Toyosu

At 5:30 PM, we found ourselves in Toyosu, enjoying a leisurely city walk through the area. We explored various shops and took in the modern urban atmosphere. For dinner, we indulged in delicious Japanese dishes, including okonomiyaki (Japanese savory pancakes), monjayaki (a type of pan-fried batter), and yakisoba (stir-fried noodles). Each dish was a delightful representation of Japanese street food, allowing us to savor the local flavors and enjoy a relaxed evening in this vibrant neighborhood.
