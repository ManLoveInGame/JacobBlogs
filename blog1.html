淺草.東京

Asakusa.Tokyo

淺草.東京 Asakusa.Tokyo

October 12TH 。 Japan 。 Tokyo 。 Asakusa

Overview

We began our Tokyo adventure at Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, Japan's oldest temple, where we enjoyed the lively atmosphere and explored Nakamise shopping street. Next, we headed to Ikebukuro, indulging in shopping at local malls and visiting Animate, a paradise for anime fans. We treated ourselves to delicious ramen before returning to Senso-ji Temple at night, where the illuminated structure offered a stunning and serene view, showcasing its beauty from a different perspective.

Tags

Japan Food See Clear Peace

9:30 AM At 淺草寺

At 9:30 AM, we arrived at Senso-ji Temple, where we began by exploring the historic exterior and admiring the traditional architecture of the surrounding streets and buildings. Afterward, we entered the temple, participating in a donation ritual by tossing coins into the offering box. We were fortunate to witness a ceremony taking place, adding to the spiritual atmosphere. Following our temple visit, we indulged in delicious street food, wandering around to sample various local delicacies and enjoy the vibrant ambiance of Asakusa.

10:15 AM Food court next to Senso-ji Temple

At 10:15 AM, we arrived at the food court next to Senso-ji Temple, eager to try various Japanese dishes. We sampled delicious yakisoba (stir-fried noodles), grilled crab sticks, takoyaki (octopus balls), saucy sausages, and refreshing shaved ice. Each dish was a delightful representation of Japanese street food, and I quickly fell in love with the flavors and textures. The vibrant atmosphere of the food court made our culinary adventure even more enjoyable, as we savored the authentic tastes of Japan right in the heart of Asakusa.

11:10 AM Shopping Street Around

At 11:10 AM, we finished our meal and strolled through the bustling shopping areas surrounding Senso-ji Temple. The streets were lined with diverse shops offering various products, showcasing the unique style and charm of Asakusa. We encountered many locals, observing their daily lives and interactions, which added to the vibrant atmosphere. The blend of traditional and modern elements in the area was fascinating, making our exploration a memorable experience.

4:15 PM Exploring Ikebukuro's Modern Culture

At 4:15 AM, we arrived in Ikebukuro to explore one of its bustling shopping malls, eager to experience contemporary Japanese culture. As we wandered through the vibrant shops, we marveled at the latest trends and popular products that define modern Japan. Our next stop was Animate, where we immersed ourselves in the world of anime and manga, reflecting the interests of Japan's youth. After a day of exploration, we capped off our adventure with a delicious bowl of ramen at night, savoring the rich flavors that left us wanting more.

9:50 PM Night Visit to Senso-ji Temple

At 9:50 PM, we returned to Senso-ji Temple to experience its enchanting night view, free from the daytime crowds. The temple, beautifully illuminated, showcased a different kind of beauty under the night sky. We strolled along Nakamise Street, where the closed shops displayed stunning street art on their shutters, adding to the serene atmosphere. The peacefulness of the area allowed us to appreciate the temple's grandeur and reflect on its historical significance in a tranquil setting. This nighttime visit offered a unique perspective of Senso-ji, making it a memorable highlight of our trip.
