Useful tips

台灣

01
Kaohsiung,Taiwan
#Travel
Explore the Night Markets

Kaohsiung's night markets, especially Liuhe Night Market, offer a vibrant atmosphere and diverse local delicacies like papaya milk and oyster omelets.

02
Kaohsiung,Taiwan
#Travel
Utilize Public Transportation

Take advantage of the efficient MRT and light rail systems to navigate the city easily, making your travel experience smooth and enjoyable.

03
Kaohsiung,Taiwan
#Travel
Visit Cijin Island

A short ferry ride from Kaohsiung, Cijin Island is perfect for a day trip. Enjoy its beaches, historical sites, and fresh seafood.

04
Kaohsiung,Taiwan
#Travel
Discover Local Cuisine

Don’t miss trying traditional dishes such as beef noodle soup and duck rice at local eateries to truly experience Kaohsiung’s culinary scene.

05
Kaohsiung,Taiwan
#Travel
Experience Cultural Sites

Visit iconic spots like the Lotus Pond and Fo Guang Shan Monastery to immerse yourself in the rich cultural heritage of Kaohsiung.

日本

01
Tokyo,Japan
#Travel
Public Transport Mastery

Utilize Tokyo's efficient train and subway system with a Suica or Pasmo card for easy navigation.

02
Tokyo,Japan
#Travel
Visit Early

Explore popular landmarks like Senso-ji Temple and Tokyo Skytree early in the morning to avoid crowds.

03
Tokyo,Japan
#Travel
Diverse Neighborhoods

Discover unique experiences in Harajuku, Akihabara, and Shibuya, each offering distinct cultural vibes.

04
Tokyo,Japan
#Travel
Savor Local Cuisine

Try sushi at Tsukiji Outer Market, ramen in Shinjuku, and street food at various night markets.

05
Tokyo,Japan
#Travel
Cultural Experiences

Visit Meiji Shrine and Yoyogi Park for a peaceful escape and to immerse yourself in Japanese culture.

01
Kyoto,Japan
#Travel
Plan Temple Visits Wisely

Kyoto is home to numerous temples. Prioritize must-see sites like Kinkaku-ji (Golden Pavilion) and Fushimi Inari-taisha, visiting early to avoid crowds.

02
Kyoto,Japan
#Travel
Take Advantage of Bicycles

Renting a bicycle is an excellent way to explore Kyoto's scenic spots at your own pace, especially along the Philosopher's Path.

03
Kyoto,Japan
#Travel
Try Traditional Kaiseki Cuisine

Experience authentic Japanese dining by indulging in kaiseki, a multi-course meal that showcases seasonal ingredients and culinary artistry.

04
Kyoto,Japan
#Travel
Attend a Tea Ceremony

Participate in a traditional tea ceremony to learn about Japanese culture and savor matcha tea in a serene setting.

05
Kyoto,Japan
#Travel
Visit During Cherry Blossom Season

If possible, plan your visit during spring to witness Kyoto's stunning cherry blossoms, particularly at Maruyama Park and along the riverbanks.
